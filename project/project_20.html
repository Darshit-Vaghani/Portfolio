Overview

CloudCost Automation is an IoT smart load control solution featuring a master-slave ESP32 design with RF communication, cloud-free storage on Google Drive via Apps Script, and offline fallback via Bluetooth. Ideal for cost-effective, scalable home and industrial automation.
